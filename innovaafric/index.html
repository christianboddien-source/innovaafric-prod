How InnovaAFRIC Works

One account unlocks the entire ecosystem — payments, shopping, delivery, and business tools.

1

Create Your Account

Sign up in under 2 minutes. One profile gives you access to all InnovaAFRIC products with a single login.

2

Verify Your Identity

Complete KYC to unlock higher limits. Supported: national ID, passport, and utility bill verification.

3

Fund Your Wallet

Shop, Send & Deliver

Send money to family, shop international products, or run your business — all in one connected platform.
