POLE 1 · SUPPLIER
Main E-Commerce Supplier

Asia — The Factory of the World

Asia is the engine of global production. Through InnovaAFRIC, African consumers access millions of products from Chinese and Southeast Asian manufacturers at competitive prices — previously impossible without complex intermediaries.

  • AliExpress & Alibaba direct integration via XenderShop
  • Electronics, fashion, home, tools, health — millions of SKUs
  • Competitive wholesale prices for B2B buyers on XenderBigShop
  • Consolidated shipping through European quality control hub
  • Returns and disputes managed by InnovaAFRIC guarantee
🇨🇳 China🇯🇵 Japan🇰🇷 Korea🇻🇳 Vietnam🇮🇳 India🇹🇭 Thailand

POLE 2 · HUB
Connection, Quality Control & Logistics

Europe — The Strategic Hub

Europe is the nerve centre of the ecosystem: international payments, diaspora services, quality certification and logistics coordination between Asia and Africa. It is also the gateway for the millions of Africans living in Europe who send money home every day.

  • Stripe card payments (Visa/Mastercard) for diaspora users
  • SEPA bank transfers — Europe to Africa in seconds
  • Product quality control & EU compliance before Africa dispatch
  • Logistics coordination: Asia → Europe → Africa supply chain
  • LATAM gateway: diaspora bridge to Latin American markets
🇪🇸 Spain🇫🇷 France🇵🇹 Portugal🇧🇪 Belgium🇮🇹 Italy🇩🇪 Germany

POLE 3 · MARKET
Consumer Potential + Producer Ecosystem

Africa — 1.4 Billion People, Infinite Opportunity

Africa is not just the end market — it is a producer, an innovator, and a growing middle class hungry for quality products and financial tools. With the highest Mobile Money penetration on the planet, Africa is ready for digital commerce at scale.
